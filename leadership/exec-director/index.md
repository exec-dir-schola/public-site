title: Daniel Morris, <i>Executive Director</i>
weight: 3
---

Dan Morris joined Schola Cantorum as Executive Director in 2018, after a long career in church music, as well as being the Liturgical Director at St. Joseph's of Cupertino Catholic Church. He is also a singer and actor, with many leading roles in opera and musical theater in his background. While in college, Dan was a member of Schola Cantorum and sang in college groups with Schola's founder, Royal Stanton, so he has an affinity for and love of beautiful choral music.
